Market Analysis- Business Decision Research

This is an analysis of potential customers to churn. In this analysis, I try to identify customers who have the potential to churn. The dataset is provided by DQLab. Data exploration is performed to get insight from the dataset. The number of transactions by year, churn proportion by product, and customer distribution are visualized clearly. Finally, machine learning model is built and tested to get the performance of the model.
